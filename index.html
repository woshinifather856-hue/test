GRISWOLD ET AL. V. CONNECTICUT, 381 U.S. 479 (1965), Supreme Court of the United States.
Appeal from the Supreme Court of Errors of Connecticut; lower courts affirmed appellants’ convictions; Supreme Court noted probable jurisdiction (379 U.S. 926).
Appellants Griswold (Planned Parenthood director) and Buxton (physician) provided contraceptive advice to married couples; arrested under Conn. Gen. Stat. §53-32 and §54-196; fined $100 each.
Whether Connecticut’s anti-contraceptive statutes violate the Due Process Clause of the Fourteenth Amendment by infringing a fundamental right to marital privacy.
Held: Connecticut’s statutes are unconstitutional, as they invade the zone of marital privacy protected by penumbras of the First, Third, Fourth, Fifth, and Ninth Amendments.
The Court relied on Meyer v. Nebraska (1923) and Pierce v. Society of Sisters (1925) to argue “fundamental liberties” include marital privacy; held state’s interest in curbing extramarital relations did not justify sweeping infringement of marital privacy.
Justice Goldberg (joined by Chief Justice and Justice Brennan) emphasized the Ninth Amendment (“rights retained by the people”) as additional support for marital privacy, beyond penumbras.
Justice Black argued there is no “general right to privacy” in the Constitution; criticized the majority for using “penumbra” to expand judicial power beyond textual limits.
Cited Truax v. Raich (1915) (standing to assert others’ rights), NAACP v. Alabama (1958) (First Amendment penumbra), Skinner v. Oklahoma (1942) (fundamental personal liberties).
Reversed the judgment of the Supreme Court of Errors of Connecticut; vacated appellants’ convictions.

2. 双语法律术语学习（点击卡片翻转）

Substantive Due Process
实体正当程序：宪法第十四修正案中关于政府行为必须符合基本公平和正义，不能侵犯公民基本权利的原则，关注法律内容本身的合理性。
Penumbra
半影：宪法未明确列举但隐含的权利，由明确权利的 “边缘” 推导而来，如隐私权的推导。
Standing
诉讼资格：当事人必须证明自己因案件结果而受到实际损害，才有资格提起诉讼。
Marital Privacy
婚姻隐私：夫妻在婚姻关系中享有的隐私权利，如生育自主、家庭生活不受政府过度干预等。
Fourteenth Amendment
第十四修正案：美国宪法第十四修正案，核心内容包括 “正当程序条款” 与 “平等保护条款”，限制州政府权力。
Strict Scrutiny
严格审查：司法审查的高标准，政府必须证明其行为是为了实现国家重大利益且手段必要。
